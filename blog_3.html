The concept of 50 + 15 System is very similar to the popular time management technique called Pomodoro. But with a couple of differences that make it 2x better.


What is the Pomodoro Technique?

    It's when you break down work into intervals.

  • 1. Work/Code for 25 minutes
  • 2. Rest for 5 minutes
  • 3. Repeat until exhausted

I've tried it. And at most, I could do 4 hours of deep work in a day.

It's not a bad result. But I always felt that I could do more. There was more locked inside me.

And it was true.

After applying the 50 + 15 System, I can perform 6-8 hours of deep work/learning every day.

And you could too.

Nothing complex. Doesn't take up much time. And gives you so much in return. Here it is.
